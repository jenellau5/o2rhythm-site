Walking Regulation • 3 min

Use: on break • after a rough interaction • before going home (dispatch conference demo)

Walk at a steady pace until the timer ends. There are two modes:

  • Audio: the walking music will play and the timer follows the duration you pick – the music stops when the clock reaches zero.
  • No audio: the music is suppressed and the Booth Scan checklist appears below. Check off items as you move until the timer expires.

Choose a length (1‑3 min) before you start; you can switch modes at any time. A stop button shows once you start so you can end early, and the timer works in both modes.
