O₂ RHYTHM

Calm — Sigh Reset

3 quiet cycles. Use when breathing feels tight or reactive.

Cycle 1

Inhale gently through the nose.

Take a short top-up sip of air.

Slowly exhale through the mouth.

Cycle 2

Repeat the same breath.

Cycle 3

Repeat once more.

Let your breathing return to normal.
