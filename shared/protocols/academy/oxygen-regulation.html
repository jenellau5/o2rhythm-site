O₂ RHYTHM

Oxygen Regulation • 4 in / 6 out

60 sec • steady exhale reset

Use: before shift • after tension spikes • before contact or tough conversations

No‑audio guide
Inhale gently through your nose for a slow count of 4. Then exhale through your mouth for a slow count of 6. Stay with that 4‑in / 6‑out rhythm for the full minute. If your shoulders are up, let them drop a notch on each exhale.
What this regulation is for: This is a quick way to take the edge off without going groggy. A slightly longer exhale helps your body switch out of fight‑or‑flight and back toward “steady,” so you can step into the next contact with a clearer head.[web:8][web:110]
