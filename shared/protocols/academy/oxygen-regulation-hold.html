O₂ RHYTHM

Oxygen Regulation • Hold Pattern

45 sec • 4 in / 2 hold / 4 out / 2 hold

Use: right after a spike • before you say or send something • before walking into contact

0:45
READY
No‑audio guide
Inhale through your nose for a count of 4. Hold your breath for 2. Exhale through your mouth for 4. Hold empty for 2. Repeat that 4 / 2 / 4 / 2 pattern until the timer ends. As you hit each hold, notice your shoulders and jaw and let them soften a notch.
What this regulation is for: This is a short “hard brake” for your system. The brief holds at the top and bottom of the breath help interrupt a surge of adrenaline or anger, so you’re less likely to react on impulse and more able to choose your next move.
