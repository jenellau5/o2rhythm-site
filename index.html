How it fits into a shift

O₂ Rhythm lives inside operations, not next to them. Crews do not leave the floor, log into an app or sit through a long session. They scan, run a short protocol and go back to work.

  • Placement plan matched to your environments and workflow.
  • Menus tuned to high acuity calls, stacked workload and post incident reset.
  • Short drills that can be run solo or as part of briefing, debrief or training blocks.
