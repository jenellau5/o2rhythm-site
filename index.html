FAQ

Clear answers. No fluff.

How is pricing determined?

Pricing is based on scope: number of environments (desk/bay/unit/etc.), number of QR placements, and your update cadence tier (monthly vs quarterly). Larger deployments and custom requests change scope.

Is it customizable for my agency/team?

Yes. Menus are built around your environments and your operational reality. Dispatch looks different than patrol; EMS looks different than corrections. The system stays the same — the menus change.

What is founder pricing?

Agencies that join during the pilot phase can lock in founder pricing when they continue after the pilot. That rate stays in place as long as you remain active on the same tier/scope.

How often do you update content?

Updates are provided monthly or quarterly depending on tier. If something operational changes (policy, environment, critical incident need), additional updates can be made when needed.

Is this “oxygen training”?

No. This is about downshift control and autonomic flexibility under stress — short protocols people can repeat inside real operations.

Do people need an app or login?

No. QR → page opens. That’s the point.

How do you keep it from becoming ignored wall clutter?

Short protocols + environment relevance + champions + measurable usage. If adoption is low, you’ll see it fast and adjust placement/menu.
