The problem

Most people do not need another wellness app. They need support in the exact moment they are trying to get through.

O₂ Rhythm gives people somewhere to pause, process, and feel less alone without needing to explain everything out loud.

When you are overwhelmed

New motherhood, parenting, caregiving, school stress, work pressure, practice, or too much at once.

When you are grieving

Loss, hospital waiting rooms, anniversaries, hard news, or the quiet after everyone else moves on.

When trust breaks

Divorce, betrayal, relationship endings, family conflict, or rebuilding after someone hurt you.

When the job follows you home

Dispatchers, nurses, first responders, teachers, corrections, healthcare workers, and anyone carrying hard moments from work.
